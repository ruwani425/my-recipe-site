Steak with Vegetables

Ingredients

  • 1 beef steak (ribeye, sirloin, or filet mignon)
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • Salt and black pepper to taste
  • 1 tsp garlic powder
  • 1 tsp dried rosemary or thyme
  • 1/2 cup cherry tomatoes
  • 1/2 cup bell peppers (sliced)
  • 1/2 cup broccoli florets
  • 1 tbsp butter
  • 2 cloves garlic (minced)

Steps to Make Steak with Vegetables

  • Preheat a skillet or grill pan over medium-high heat.
  • Rub the steak with olive oil, salt, black pepper, garlic powder, and rosemary.
  • Cook the steak for 3-5 minutes per side (or until desired doneness).
  • Remove from heat and let the steak rest for 5 minutes.
  • In the same pan, add butter and minced garlic.
  • Add cherry tomatoes, bell peppers, and broccoli, sautéing for 3-5 minutes.
  • Season the vegetables with salt and pepper to taste.
  • Serve the steak with sautéed vegetables and enjoy!

Chickpea Curry

Ingredients

  • 2 cans (15 oz) chickpeas, drained and rinsed
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 large onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1-inch piece of fresh ginger, grated
  • 1 can (14 oz) diced tomatoes
  • 1 can (14 oz) coconut milk
  • 1 tbsp curry powder
  • 1 tsp cumin
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric
  • 1/2 tsp paprika
  • Salt and black pepper to taste
  • 1 tbsp lemon juice
  • Fresh cilantro, chopped (for garnish)
  • Rice or naan (for serving)

How to Make Chickpea Curry ?

  • Heat olive oil in a large pan over medium heat.
  • Add the chopped onion and saut√© until soft and golden brown (about 5-7 minutes).
  • Add garlic and ginger, cooking for another 1-2 minutes until fragrant.
  • Add the curry powder, cumin, turmeric, and paprika, and stir for 1 minute to release the spices' aroma.
  • Pour in the diced tomatoes and coconut milk, stirring to combine.
  • Add the chickpeas, salt, and pepper, and bring to a simmer.
  • Simmer for about 15-20 minutes, letting the flavors meld together and the curry thicken slightly.
  • Stir in the lemon juice and adjust the seasoning with salt and pepper if needed.
  • Serve over rice or with naan and garnish with fresh cilantro.
  • Enjoy your delicious Chickpea Curry!

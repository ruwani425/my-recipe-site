Berry Smoothie

Ingredients

  • 1 cup mixed berries (strawberries, blueberries, raspberries)
  • 1 ripe banana
  • 1 cup Greek yogurt
  • 1 tbsp honey (optional)
  • 1 cup almond milk (or milk of choice)

How to Make Berry Smoothie?

  • Add the mixed berries (strawberries, blueberries, raspberries) into a blender.
  • Peel and add 1 ripe banana for creaminess.
  • Add 1 cup of Greek yogurt for a thick and creamy texture.
  • Drizzle 1 tbsp of honey for extra sweetness (optional).
  • Pour in 1 cup of almond milk (or your choice of milk) to help blend everything smoothly.
  • Blend all ingredients on high until smooth and creamy.
  • Taste and adjust sweetness or consistency by adding more milk or honey.
  • Pour into a glass and enjoy! You can garnish with extra berries or chia seeds if desired.

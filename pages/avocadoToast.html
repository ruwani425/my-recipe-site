Avocado Toast

Ingredients

  • 2 slices of whole-grain bread
  • 1 ripe avocado
  • 1/2 tsp lemon juice
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1/4 tsp black pepper
  • 1/4 tsp red pepper flakes (optional)
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1/2 tsp garlic powder (optional)
  • 1/4 cup cherry tomatoes (sliced, optional)
  • 1 poached or fried egg (optional)
  • Fresh herbs (parsley, cilantro, or basil for garnish)

How to Make Avocado Toast ?

  • Toast the slices of whole-grain bread until golden brown.
  • In a small bowl, mash the avocado with lemon juice, salt, black pepper, and garlic powder (if using).
  • Spread the mashed avocado mixture evenly over the toasted bread.
  • Drizzle with olive oil and sprinkle red pepper flakes if desired.
  • Top with sliced cherry tomatoes and a poached or fried egg if using.
  • Garnish with fresh herbs for extra flavor.
  • Serve immediately and enjoy your delicious Avocado Toast!

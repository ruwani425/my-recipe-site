Mango Banana Smoothie

Ingredients

  • 1 ripe mango (peeled and chopped)
  • 1 ripe banana (peeled)
  • 1 cup coconut milk (or milk of choice)
  • 1 tbsp honey (optional)
  • 1 tsp chia seeds (optional, for extra nutrients)

How to Make Mango Banana Smoothie?

  • Peel and chop the ripe mango.
  • Peel and add 1 ripe banana for natural sweetness.
  • Pour in 1 cup of coconut milk (or milk of your choice).
  • Add 1 tbsp of honey for extra sweetness (optional).
  • Sprinkle 1 tsp of chia seeds for added fiber and omega-3 (optional).
  • Blend all ingredients on high until smooth and creamy.
  • If you prefer a thicker consistency, add more banana or reduce the coconut milk.
  • Pour into a glass and enjoy the tropical flavors!

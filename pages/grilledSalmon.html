Grilled Salmon

Ingredients

  • 2 salmon fillets
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tbsp lemon juice
  • 2 cloves garlic (minced)
  • 1 tsp honey
  • 1 tsp Dijon mustard
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1/2 tsp black pepper
  • 1/2 tsp paprika
  • 1 tsp dried parsley or fresh parsley (chopped)
  • 1/2 tsp chili flakes (optional)
  • Lemon wedges (for serving)

How to Make Grilled Salmon ?

  • Preheat the grill to medium-high heat (about 400°F or 200°C).
  • In a small bowl, mix olive oil, lemon juice, minced garlic, honey, Dijon mustard, salt, black pepper, paprika, and parsley.
  • Brush the marinade over the salmon fillets, ensuring even coating.
  • Place the salmon skin-side down on the grill.
  • Grill for about 4-5 minutes per side, or until the salmon flakes easily with a fork.
  • Remove from the grill and let it rest for 2 minutes.
  • Serve hot with lemon wedges and your favorite side dish.
  • Enjoy your delicious Grilled Salmon!

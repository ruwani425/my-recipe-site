Quinoa Salad

Ingredients

  • 1 cup quinoa, rinsed
  • 2 cups water or vegetable broth
  • 1 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1 cucumber, diced
  • 1/4 cup red onion, finely chopped
  • 1/4 cup feta cheese, crumbled (optional)
  • 1/4 cup olives, sliced (optional)
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tbsp lemon juice
  • 1 tsp dried oregano
  • Salt and black pepper to taste
  • Fresh parsley, chopped (for garnish)

How to Make Quinoa Salad ?

  • In a medium pot, bring the water or vegetable broth to a boil.
  • Add the quinoa, reduce the heat to low, cover, and simmer for about 15 minutes or until the quinoa is cooked and the liquid is absorbed.
  • Remove from heat and fluff the quinoa with a fork. Let it cool.
  • In a large bowl, combine the cooled quinoa with cherry tomatoes, cucumber, red onion, feta cheese, and olives.
  • In a small bowl, whisk together the olive oil, lemon juice, oregano, salt, and pepper.
  • Pour the dressing over the quinoa mixture and toss until well combined.
  • Garnish with fresh parsley and serve immediately or refrigerate for later.

Green Detox Smoothie

Ingredients

  • 1 cup spinach (fresh or frozen)
  • 1/2 cucumber (peeled and chopped)
  • 1 green apple (cored and chopped)
  • 1/2 lemon (juiced)
  • 1 tsp ginger (freshly grated)
  • 1 cup water or coconut water
  • 1-2 tsp honey or sweetener (optional)

Steps to Make Green Detox Smoothie

  • Wash and prepare the spinach, cucumber, and green apple.
  • Peel and chop 1/2 cucumber and core the green apple.
  • Juice 1/2 lemon and grate 1 tsp of fresh ginger.
  • Add all ingredients into a blender, including 1 cup of water or coconut water.
  • Blend until smooth and creamy.
  • If you prefer a sweeter smoothie, add 1-2 tsp of honey or your preferred sweetener.
  • Pour into a glass and enjoy this refreshing, nutrient-packed detox smoothie!

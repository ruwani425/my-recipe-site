Banana Oat Pancakes

Ingredients

  • 1 ripe banana
  • 1 cup rolled oats
  • 2 eggs
  • 1/2 cup milk (or almond milk)
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • 1/2 tsp cinnamon
  • 1 tbsp honey or maple syrup
  • Pinch of salt
  • Butter or oil (for cooking)
  • Fresh fruits and syrup (for serving)

How to Make Banana Oat Pancakes

  • Blend the oats in a blender until they turn into a fine flour.
  • Add the banana, eggs, milk, vanilla extract, baking powder, cinnamon, honey, and salt to the blender.
  • Blend until the batter is smooth.
  • Heat a non-stick pan over medium heat and lightly grease it with butter or oil.
  • Pour small amounts of batter onto the pan to form pancakes.
  • Cook for about 2-3 minutes until bubbles form on the surface.
  • Flip and cook for another 1-2 minutes until golden brown.
  • Repeat with the remaining batter.
  • Serve warm with fresh fruits and syrup.
  • Enjoy your healthy Banana Oat Pancakes!

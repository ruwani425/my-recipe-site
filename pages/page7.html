Turkey Sandwich

Ingredients

  • 2 slices whole-grain bread
  • 4 oz sliced turkey breast
  • 1 slice Swiss or cheddar cheese
  • 2 lettuce leaves
  • 2 tomato slices
  • 1 tbsp mayonnaise or mustard
  • 1 tbsp butter (for toasting, optional)

How to Make Turkey Sandwich ?

  • Spread mayonnaise or mustard on one side of each slice of bread.
  • Layer turkey slices evenly over one slice of bread.
  • Add a slice of cheese, followed by lettuce and tomato slices.
  • Top with the second slice of bread.
  • For a toasted sandwich, butter the outer sides and grill in a pan over medium heat until golden brown.
  • Slice in half and enjoy your delicious turkey sandwich!

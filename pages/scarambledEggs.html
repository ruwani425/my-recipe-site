Scrambled Eggs

Ingredients

  • 3 large eggs
  • 2 tbsp milk (optional, for creamier texture)
  • Salt and black pepper to taste
  • 1 tbsp butter or olive oil
  • 1 tbsp chopped fresh chives (optional)
  • 1/4 cup shredded cheese (optional)

How to Make Scrambled Eggs ?

  • Crack the eggs into a bowl and whisk until fully combined.
  • Add milk (if using), salt, and black pepper, then mix well.
  • Heat a non-stick pan over low to medium heat and add butter or olive oil.
  • Pour in the egg mixture and let it sit undisturbed for a few seconds.
  • Gently stir with a spatula, folding the eggs as they cook.
  • Cook until the eggs are soft and slightly runny or to your desired consistency.
  • Remove from heat and sprinkle with fresh chives and cheese if desired.
  • Serve immediately and enjoy your fluffy scrambled eggs!
